Instructions

For Students:

  • Register yourself by providing correct details on the Registration page.
  • Login using your registered Email ID, Password.
  • Fill out the Leave Application form to apply for leave.
  • You can view your leave history and check the status of your applications.
  • Make sure to logout after your session for security.

For Faculty:

  • Register yourself by providing correct details on the Registration page(Faculty role will be assigned by Admin).
  • Login using your registered Email ID and Password, selecting your role as Faculty.
  • View all pending leave applications submitted by students for your approval.
  • While approving, you can also view the leave history of the student.
  • Approve or reject leave applications based on your discretion.
  • Remember to logout after completing your tasks.
